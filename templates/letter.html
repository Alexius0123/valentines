Dear Love,

I can hardly believe how far we've come together. From late-night Costco runs in California to wild parties in Mexico, from the bright lights of New York to day drinking in New Orleans, from savoury BBQ in Texas to finding comfort in Bali, and from exploring Singapore and Hong Kong to embracing the chilly winter nights in Korea. It's amazing to think that it is already been almost a year since the world decided two crazy, unhinged people who met on the other side of the Earth should be together. We have had our ups and downs, but more than anything, I am glad we have made it this far. There are times I wish you were right here beside me, and in those moments, it takes all my self-control not to book a flight on the spot. The distance is hard—for both of us—but at least we are never more than one phone call away. I hope you know that from the bottom of my heart, I always care about you, even though sometimes I may not show it, even when we fight, even when we are both too busy and swarmed to have a long conversation. And most importantly, I just wanted to tell you this Valentine's, that I will always try my best to be there for you. I love you.

From a boy just 2,500km away,
Your Bubs
